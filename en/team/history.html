The team

We are 70 students from the University of Padova, chosen through a careful and targeted recruiting. Our team is based on values ​​such as collaboration, involvement, willingness to share and learn. Race Up members develop cross-disciplinary skills such as teamwork, project planning and problem solving; skills that will prove to be fundamental in the job market . We believe that only with this environment we can achieve the goals we set for each season. Our commitment continues beyond competitions through a constant research that leads to innovation in materials and working techniques. The desire to innovate pushed the team to introduce, in addition to the internal combustion engine, a second wholly electric car. This category was recently introduced in Formula SAE, resulting in the first Italian team to embark on this ambitious project.

Competitions

  • Design: during the initial phase of the project, technical department members design using advanced professional software such as 3D CAD design development tools, Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and CFD analysis.

  • Production: after finishing the first phase, the two cars will come into the workshop, using both basic and more specific tools, such as lathes and mills, but also welders (MIG, TIG) and corner grinders.
    The Business & Marketing team, on the other hand, contributes to the development of business relations, communication and economic and financial planning for each of the two cars.
