Race UP is one of the most important Formula Student academic team: since 2006, more than a hundred male and female students of the Patavina University, from Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace, Electronics, Mechatronics, Management, Economics and Communication Sciences, merge expertise and passion in training a successful experience.


Formula SAE is a competition between university students organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) which provides for the design and production of a racing car, evaluated in a series of tests based on its quality of design and efficiency of ingegneristica.Istituita in 1981, the competition has now spread to all over the world, with ten annual events organized directly by the SAE in cooperation with the national associations of engineers and technicians car.
