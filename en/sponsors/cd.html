Sponsors in partnership with Race Up Combustion Division

Thanks to a group of loyal partners and to the valuable, economic and professional contributions, Race UP project goes on and keeps improving every year. Thanks to their help, Race up grows and improves car performance. Our gratitude goes to:

The University of Padua is an Italian statal university founded in 1222, one of the oldest in the world. The University of Padua is one of the paradigmatic cases of the medieval University born from the migration of a group of students from the pre-existing university of Bologna.
OZ Racing is born from an authentic passion for the wheels. Founded in 1971, it is a legendary company in the automotive world. OZ in forty years has worked with the most exclusive automakers: Ferrari, Lamborghini, Maserati, Aston Martin and Lotus, McLaren. Collaborations that gave birth to exclusive wheels, carefully preserved in the museum in the headquarters of San Martino di Lupari in Padua.
Compositex deals with materials processing, competition cars’ frame construction; construction of composite details for aeronautical, automotive, nautical areas, cycling, motorcyclists and in general for competitions; repair of scooters and pieces in composite materials for various sectors; commercialization of particulars in composite materials.
Founded in 1969 in Schio (Vicenza), S.Ti.P. Srl it is a highly qualified model for the production of metal molds and boasts considerable experience in the construction of models for Engine Bases, Engine Heads, Gearboxes, Axles and Differential Boxes.
Metalvenice Srl realizes painting on fiber and composite fiber for big brands of MADE IN ITALY. Passion for work, attention to customers and production problems, always and in any case, when challenged by technical obstacles. This is the philosophy of the Venetian company that is "customer partner".
Established in 1973, Meccanica Padovan provides special components and works with some of the most powerful teams in Motorsport. The continuous research of new technologies to fulfil the expectations of the teams has led the company in many collaborations with some of the biggest brands in the automotive panorama.
Rossi Racing, was established in 2002 from the passion of the founder Gianluca Rossi for the Motorsport world. He decided to fully dedicate himself to the company after gaining experience in the automotive world. Rossi Racing collaborated with many important teams (such as Husqvarna Motorcycles and Reitwagen) to develop racing engines that later achieved the expected results. In 2011 entered in the AMA championship with KTM LC8 and in Coppa Italia with a BMW E46, collecting good results.
ZF is one of the most important technology groups in the world. It works in the transmission and chassis system sectors, and also in the active and passive technology security. ZF have operated in Italy since 1951: now it is a Alfa Romeo supplier of “Giulietta” first equipment for steering system. Main ZF business italian areas are: automotive sector, industrial automotive sector, marine sector, railway and wind industry.
