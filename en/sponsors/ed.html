Sponsors in partnership with Race Up Electric Division

Thanks to a group of loyal partners and to the valuable, economic and professional contributions, Race Up project goes on and keeps improving every year. Thanks to their help, Race up grows and improves car performance. Our gratitude goes to:

OZ Racing is born from an authentic passion for the wheels. Founded in 1971, it is a legendary company in the automotive world. OZ in forty years has worked with the most exclusive automakers: Ferrari, Lamborghini, Maserati, Aston Martin and Lotus, McLaren. Collaborations that gave birth to exclusive wheels, carefully preserved in the museum in the headquarters of San Martino di Lupari in Padua.
Compositex deals with materials processing, competition cars’ frame construction; construction of composite details for aeronautical, automotive, nautical areas, cycling, motorcyclists and in general for competitions; repair of scooters and pieces in composite materials for various sectors; commercialization of particulars in composite materials.
Efesto Lab is a company dedicated to SERVICE in 3D PRINTING with the aim to continually seek new developments in order to expand the quality and the offer of additive manufacturing.
HT was born in 1983. At the beginning it marketed inspection equipment, pliers and multimeters. In 1992 HT, thanks to experiences and ideas gained during the years, fixed an important goal: project and realise their own tools. In 2001 the company launch the first tools on the market that checked electrical system security and also did a single-phase and a three-phase quality analysis of the electrical service supplying (GSC family). Today this tools are instrumentation icons.
Lamiertek srl was born thanks to the founders’ alliance, experience and passion. The company deals with steel, inox steel, aluminum and copper processing and her mission consist of satisfying clients’ needs and producing a worth and quality product. Lamiertek’s goal is finding technical and innovative solutions to allow customer to be more competitive and advantaged.
CPS realise reliable product thanks to the constant commitment, constant research and the commitment to achieving the highest quality. Most advanced and sophisticated technologies are used throughout the production cycle, from design (with CAD/CAM) to the construction and control ( with CN machines).
DORFA SpA works in the precision mechanics for more than 40 years. It is a qualified and reliable partner that guarantee the production of complex mechanical details for developing a business.
Company that project and produce molds for die casting, sheet metal stamping, assembly, chip removals processing and padding.
Melasta Corporation is one of the largest rechargeable batteries producer and providers. Thanks to the success of their lithium polymer batteries (LifePo4, Li-ion and NIMH) Melasta gained a great location inside global market.
Simpa was born in 1958 and today is a leader in profiles for aluminum frames sector and in the most demanding customer service for extrusion in general field.
MPZ srl was born in 2006 when the founder Gianni Zanini, that already had a thirty years professional experience in the mechanics field, started their own precision mechanics business for third parties.
ZF is one of the most important technology groups in the world. It works in the transmission and chassis system sectors, and also in the active and passive technology security. ZF have operated in Italy since 1951: now it is a Alfa Romeo supplier of “Giulietta” first equipment for steering system. Main ZF business italian areas are: automotive sector, industrial automotive sector, marine sector, railway and wind industry.
Metal Bushings Italia was born in 1989 and works in the manufacturing, mechanics and automotive sectors.
Sika is a swiss company, that was born in 1910 in Baar. Its industrial sector, Sika Industry, develops solutions for bonding, sealing, isolation, soundproofing and reinforcement in the automotive and nautical sectors. Today is one of the most important world supplier in these areas.
The company works in the carpentry and floor covering sectors.
Molex was born in 1938. Its head office is in Illinois (USA) and it is actually the second best electric, electronic and optical connection producer of the world.
ODU is one of the leader in electrical connection system supply thanks to the efficiency and customer strategies. Its technology guarantees an optimal power, signal and information transfer specially in the energy and automotive fields. Head office’s company is in Germany, but there are many factories over the world.
OMP is a world leader business in the clothing design and production sectors for Motorsport and Karting. It was born in 1973 in Genova, and it has grown over the years thanks to their products success. Today is one of the few world company that offer a comprehensive range of all the security products, for the drivers and teams but also for the car setup, in the automotive and kart competitions.
RS works in various fields: electronic components, connectors, cables, electricity, automation, tools, mechanics, information technology, instrumentation and security.
Texas instruments is a US company in Dallas. It is one of the 10 most important semiconductors developers and producers and it has production, development and marketing establishments in 3 different continents. The company goal is the analog chips, embedded processors and education technology development.
TME Electronic Components is one of the main distributors of electronic components, electrotechnical components and workshop and laboratory equipments in the European market.
Sorma is one of the most important competitors in international markets. It has 2 division: the first works in brand names delivery for chip removal field and the other one works in abrasive and superabrasives tools production and delivery (Diamante & CBN).
The company produces molds for plastic molding.
Since 1972 our company works in precision mechanics and carpentry branches, helping the client in all his productive needs: turning, milling, welding, laser cutting and metal sheet manufacturing.
Elpav Instruments is a leader in customized electronic instruments delivery . It operates in many fields that concerned technology of electric and electronic measurements.
It is a leader company in the motorcycle industry thanks to a long and consolidated experience in many important supplies to the main motorcycles and scooter manufacturers of the world.
Its main product is the multilayer but it also produces IMS printed circuits, traditional single-layer and double-layer, SMD steel sheets, high frequency circuits and components assembly.
Today Knipex is the leader pliers brand in the world. Its users came from over 100 countries and the trend is increasing.
The “Nuova Carrozzeria Stile” is a young and dynamic company. It was born in 2003 and offers several services such as: car bodywork, painting mechanical assistance, car coupons, tires repair, car review, restoring highlights, car rent, blue note and roadside assistance.
