Origin-E

The rigidity of the single-seater and above all the safety of the rider is guaranteed by the carbon fiber monocoque we have developed, with a weight of only 23 kg allowing us to be both powerful and safe at the same time.
We have fully developed the entire electronic compartment by also providing traction control, regenerative braking and torque vectoring on the 4 wheels.

Origin-E 2016

Production year: 2016
Weight: 200 kg
Acceleration 0-100 km/h: <3 s
Power: 4 x 37 kW
